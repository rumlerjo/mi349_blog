About me!

Short bio

Hi there! I'm John Rumler, and this is my blog. I'm a 4th year student at Michigan State University pursuing a
Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Engineering. I have always been fascinated by computers, but even as
the childlike wonder of the internet and videogames has become a more tangible subject, I've maintained an
interest and continue to learn more. After college I am mostly interested in working with software development,
preferably at a firm with a more specialized clientele, but in my free time I have a passion for games development
as a hobby, so far mostly in 2d/text but working towards 3d as I find time (my modelling skills are abysmal).
